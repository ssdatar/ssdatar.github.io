About me

Work Experience

I graduated from Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology, Nagpur in 2009 with a degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering. But I decided to put my skills to use for a different form of communication - journalism.

I joined Just Another Magazine (JAM) as writer, where I got to write on a wide variety of topics like education, technology, entrepreneurship, and urban issues.

My last job was at Mid-day , where I was on the copy desk. Apart from my copy editing and page-making duties, I did features reporting for the Sunday edition. I also produced the paper's first interactive pieces, which, I believe, was a first for any news organization in Mumbai. That was fun.

I also worked in advertising at Ogilvy and 93.5 Red FM , a radio station in Mumbai.

I received a tuition fellowship from Stanford University for their master's program in journalism. I am also a J N Tata scholar , K C Mahindra scholar , and a recipient of scholarship grants from South Asian Journalists Association and Asian American Journalists Association .
