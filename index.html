Criminal Lawmakers

Mid-day Data investigation of Maharashtra's lawmakers with criminal backgrounds

This was the first data journalism/interactive story for the paper. The state of Maharashtra, India went to the polls in 2014. Each candidate is required to file an affidavit with the election commission, detailing his finances and any criminal cases against him/her.

Unfortunately, these are all scanned images - which a small non-profit managed to convert to PDFs. I scraped these PDFs using Tabula and analyzed the criminal records of the elected lawmakers, and found that nearly 60 percent of them had criminal charges - some had been charged for kidnapping, robbery and attempt to murder.

I built interactive charts allowing people to explore this data.

Mumbai's tainted lawmakers
